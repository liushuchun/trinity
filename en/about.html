We provide asset valuation, financial advisory, due diligence, financing and M&A services across diverse industries and our clients are mainly comprised of listed companies and listing candidates in China, HK and US, as well as large private corporations, state-owned enterprises and multinationals.

Asia (Beijing) Asset Appraisal Co., Ltd. ("Asia Appraisal"), a member firm of Trinity Group, was originally founded in 1993. It is the first batch of firms qualified for asset appraisal of securities and futures related business. Asia Appraisal is now a holder of China Asset Appraisal Qualification (issued by Minister of Finance) and Asset Appraisal Qualification for Business related to Securities and Futures (jointly issued by Minister of Finance and China Securities Regulatory Commission).

Our analysts provide customised services to Chinese companies with global perspective and sharp perception of local markets.
